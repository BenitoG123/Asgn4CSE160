Controls:
- Walk around with WASD
- Fly up with I or space
- Fly down with left shift
- Turn camera with Q and E or click and drag mouse
- Place block with J
- Delete block with K
- Choose block texture with number 1, 2, 3, or 4


Story:
This is a small island that fisherman like to stop by and dock.
It is said that a fisherman found the island while reeling in an octopus.
He would then have the best haul he ever had, and this island was deemed good luck.
Today there is a fountain with an octopus on it to wish for the island's luck for any fisherman passing by.
