notes for grader:

It is not optimized, so it can lag but it should work otherwise.

Slider Directions:
-Light is the specular + diffuse + ambient light.
This can be turned on and off and moved around with the Light X,Y,Z sliders.
-Spotlight is the spotlight.
This can be moved around with Spotlight Eye X,Y,Z sliders. You can change where it is looking with the Spotlight Direction sliders
Both the light and spot light positions are rendered as small cubes.
-Spotlight Fade is how quickly the spotlight turns to black as it goes out from the center
-Spotlight Angle is the max angle of light that can come from the spotlight
note: the direction sliders isn't an "At" point, it is the vector direction
note: I made it so the light and spotlight can't both be on, so to turn all the lights off you only need to turn off the one that is currently on.

Controls:
- Walk around with WASD
- Fly up with I or space
- Fly down with left shift
- Turn camera with Q and E or click and drag mouse
- Place block with J
- Delete block with K
- Choose block texture with number 1, 2, 3, or 4
